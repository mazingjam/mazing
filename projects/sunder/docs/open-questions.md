# Open Questions

## Player Count

- What is the target player count?
- Should solo be a first-class mode?
- Does each player control one hero, or can solo/two-player games control multiple heroes?

## Turn Structure

- Do all players act in any order during a shared round?
- Do enemies act after all players, after each player, or according to encounter scripts?
- Does energy refresh every round, every page, or through hero-specific rules?

## Energy Economy

- How much base energy should a hero provide?
- Are energy colors fixed on the hero, rolled, drawn, or chosen?
- Can unused energy carry over?
- Are cards that generate energy temporary, persistent, or exhaustible?

## Deckbuilding

- Are cards colorless but cost colored energy, or do cards also have color identity?
- Can any hero draft any card?
- Should black cards be rare, corruptive, cursed, or simply specialized?

## Book Map

- How many spreads are in a run?
- Are pages fixed, shuffled, slotted into a binder, or unlocked as packets?
- How much hidden information should page-turning create?
- Can party members occupy different pages, or only different nodes on one spread?

## Combat Scope

- Is combat abstract like Slay the Spire, spatial like a tactical board, or hybrid?
- Do enemies have decks, scripts, intent cards, dice, or fixed behavior?
- Are positions represented on the book page?
