# Glossary

## Energy

The spendable resource used to play cards and activate some effects.

- Red: brute force, blunt action, damage, directness, tempo.
- Green: support, noncombatants, protection, growth, healing, companions, positioning.
- Blue: cunning, spells, manipulation, card flow, timing, tricks.
- White: flexible energy that can pay for any color.
- Black: restricted energy that can only pay black costs.

## Hero Card

The primary player identity card. It provides base energy each turn and likely defines starting deck, health, traits, and special rules.

## Book Map

The physical board structure. Each open spread of the book forms the active map area, with printed locations, encounters, routes, opportunities, and page-specific rules.

## Spread

Two facing pages in the book. A spread is the current playable map state.

## Encounter

A combat, event, hazard, social scene, reward, shop, camp, boss, or other playable node on a book spread.

## Split Party

A state where players choose different branches or locations on the same spread or across connected spreads. This should create tactical and reward tradeoffs.
