# Image Prompts

Alla bilder genererades med built-in `image_gen` och kopierades sedan in i projektet. De ar forsta utkast, inte slutlig tryckfardig illustration.

## Cover

Use case: illustration-story  
Asset type: children's board book cover illustration  
Primary request: Safer revised cover art for a personalized first baby bedtime book, no visible text.  
Scene/backdrop: cozy Scandinavian nursery at night, crib with firm flat mattress, window with moon and a few stars, gentle warm lamp glow.  
Subject: a calm newborn baby lying safely on their back in a fitted sleep sack on a firm crib mattress; no pillow, no loose blanket, no toys inside the crib. Baby has simple customizable features, warm inclusive appearance, no specific ethnicity emphasized. A small plush star may sit on a shelf outside the crib, clearly not in the sleep area.  
Style/medium: soft hand-painted watercolor and colored pencil children's book illustration, premium Scandinavian baby brand feeling.  
Composition/framing: portrait cover composition, baby and moon as focal points, generous calm negative space at top for title added later.  
Lighting/mood: sleepy, tender, safe, magical but grounded.  
Color palette: warm cream, muted sage, soft sky blue, pale yellow moonlight, gentle coral accents.  
Constraints: no text, no watermark, no logos, no pillow, no loose blanket, no toys in crib, no unsafe sleep clutter, no photorealism, no sharp contrast.
