# Month 1 Box Prototype

Updated: 2026-06-16

## Box Name

**Forsta godnatt**

## Customer Promise

Your baby's first GrowUp box creates a calm bedtime ritual and gives the family a personal keepsake from the first month.

## Included In The Box

1. Personalized board book: `Godnatt, lilla stjarna`.
2. Milestone card: "Min forsta manad".
3. Parent note: short guidance on reading to newborns and making a repeatable evening cue.
4. App unlock: baby profile, bedtime memory and next-box preference.

## Website Preview Fields

The landing page should ask for:

- Child name.
- Birth month.
- Avatar tone.
- Hair option.
- Subscription type: own baby or gift.

The preview should update:

- Book title line.
- Dedication line.
- Box schedule starting month.
- Selected plan.

## Operational Notes

- Month 1 must be able to ship within 5-7 business days after order.
- For MVP, personalization can be applied to cover/dedication text while interior illustrations use variant pools.
- Avoid promising fully custom illustrations until generation, QA and print approval are reliable.
- The app should collect richer profile data after purchase, not block checkout.

## Success Metrics

- Landing page to preview-start rate.
- Preview-start to email capture rate.
- Email capture to checkout intent.
- Checkout conversion by own-baby vs gift.
- Month 2 selection completion rate.
