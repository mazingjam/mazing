# Archetype Staples v1

First-pass staple cards for draft archetypes.

Assumptions:

- `Gold` costs are written `{Gold}`.
- To pay `{Gold}`, remove a gold counter from a permanent you control.
- Buildings can be attacked, cannot attack, can block, and deal combat damage.
- Creature removal does not target Buildings unless they are also creatures.
- Doom triggers from anywhere and usually exiles the card after resolving.
- Bribe shorthand means any opponent may pay the cost at the relevant time; if paid, the listed bribe outcome happens.
