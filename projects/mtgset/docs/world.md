## Factions

### WBU: Old Money + Government

Themes:

- Old money.
- Government.
- Bureaucracy.
- Inherited power.
- Legal authority.
- Institutional control.

Visual anchors:

- Marble offices, ministries, courts, banks, archives, estates.
- Enchantment Buildings as laws, charters, offices, or social institutions.
- Gold as status, legitimacy, debt, taxation, and influence.

### RWU: Individualism + Military + Progress

Themes:

- Individualism.
- Military power.
- Progress.
- Ambition.
- Civic identity.
- Public heroism and national myth.

Visual anchors:

- Banners, uniforms, monuments, factories, academies, airships, public works, bright civic architecture.
- Gold as medals, funding, rank, patents, investment, and authorized advancement.

### WBR: Human Citizens

Themes:

- TBD.

- RGU.

Outliers:

Color positioning:
