# Dictation 2026-06-16 004: Pre-Skeleton Constraints

## Raw Notes

- Before generating the set, answer some structural questions.
- Goal: make three common, uncommon, and rare staples in each archetype.
- Creature removal does not target Buildings.
- Buildings almost always interact with gold or Staff.
- Buildings are otherwise relatively underpowered.
- Baseline example: a 2-cost artifact Building is around 0/4.
- Each civilization faction has a tri-land.
- Each civ tri-land enters tapped and has a gold counter interaction.

## Immediate Interpretation

- Initial card generation should be archetype-staple driven, not full skeleton random fill.
- Buildings need their mechanical value to come from gold/Staff, not raw stats.
- Creature removal should remain creature-focused, preserving Buildings as a distinct permanent class.
- Civ-faction fixing should support three-color drafting while tying into gold counters.
