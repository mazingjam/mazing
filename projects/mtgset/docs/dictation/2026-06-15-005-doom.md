# Dictation 2026-06-15 005: Doom

## Raw Notes

- Another mechanic is a doom mechanic.
- It triggers when this card is put into the graveyard.
- Doom triggers from anywhere.
- Consider whether Doom needs "Then exile this card" to prevent loops or excessive recursion.

## Immediate Interpretation

- `Doom` is a graveyard-trigger mechanic.
- It can appear on multiple card types because it triggers from anywhere.
- The mechanic should support sacrifice, combat death, discard/mill, or attrition themes depending on final wording.
- Because Doom triggers from anywhere, repeatable recursion/self-mill/discard loops are a major balance risk.

## Open Rules Questions

- Resolved: Doom triggers from anywhere.
- Does Doom trigger on tokens? Current assumption: if tokens can have Doom, they trigger when put into a graveyard before ceasing to exist.
- Is Doom an ability word, keyword, or just a recurring named trigger?
- Does Doom require a cost, or is it always a triggered ability?
- Should most Doom cards include "Then exile this card" as default safety text?
