# Dictation 2026-06-15 007: WBU Faction

## Raw Notes

- WBU is the "old money + government" guild/faction.
- WBU rewards `Wealth`.
- WBU uses gold counter interaction.
- WBU uses enchantment Buildings with "cannot block."

## Immediate Interpretation

- WBU is an Esper-colored faction: white, blue, black.
- Theme: entrenched institutions, inherited wealth, bureaucracy, social control, formal power.
- Mechanical identity:
  - Wealth threshold rewards.
  - Gold counter placement, movement, protection, and exploitation.
  - Enchantment Buildings as institutions/laws/offices/estates.
  - Some WBU Buildings intentionally cannot block, meaning they function more as systems/infrastructure than defensive structures.

## Design Notes

- "Cannot block" on Buildings is notable because Buildings normally can block. This should be used deliberately as faction identity, not as default Building behavior.
- WBU probably wants durable value engines rather than aggressive stats.
- WBU can be the faction most likely to treat gold counters as legitimacy, taxation, influence, or legal authority.
