# Dictation 2026-06-16 003: Green Structure

## Raw Notes

- Correction: RGU, not RGB.
- Black should stay with the civilization factions, even though it traditionally fits doom thematically.
- Red and blue are also nature's colors in this set.
- If mono-green, GR, and GU are all things, then RGU becomes a natural extension.
- There should still be outliers in all colors.
- GW can try to unite humanity and nature.
- GB can go hard on the Doom mechanic.

## Immediate Interpretation

- RGU is the natural three-color green/nature convergence.
- Black's main role remains in civilization factions.
- Green is not isolated; it extends through red and blue as natural forces.
- GW and GB exist as outlier archetypes, not necessarily primary draft pillars.

## Draft Structure Implication

- Primary nature lanes: mono-green, GR, GU.
- Natural three-color convergence: RGU.
- Outliers:
  - GW: unity/reconciliation between humanity and nature.
  - GB: heavy Doom/decay, likely more extreme and less central.
