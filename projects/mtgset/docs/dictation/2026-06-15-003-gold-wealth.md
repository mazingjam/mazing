# Dictation 2026-06-15 003: Gold Counters and Wealth

## Raw Notes

- Gold counters are the theme of the set.
- Connected to gold counters, there is a keyword: `Wealth X`.
- Wealth activates when you have a total amount of gold counters across permanents you control.

## Immediate Interpretation

- Gold counters are a central set resource and should likely appear across multiple colors/archetypes.
- `Wealth X` is a threshold mechanic.
- The threshold checks the total number of gold counters on permanents you control.
- Wealth can appear as an ability word or keyword depending on whether the check is only a condition or needs shared rules text.

## Open Rules Questions

- Are gold counters only placed on permanents, or can players also get gold counters?
- Do gold counters have intrinsic rules meaning, or only matter through card text and Wealth?
- Does `Wealth X` mean "as long as permanents you control have X or more gold counters among them"?
- Does Wealth turn abilities on continuously, trigger once, or modify spells/abilities at resolution?
