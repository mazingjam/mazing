# Dictation 2026-06-15 008: RWU Faction

## Raw Notes

- RWU is the "America" guild/faction.
- Themes: individualism, military, progress.
- It uses a mix of the new mechanics.
- A clear theme is that things with gold counters on them become better.

## Immediate Interpretation

- RWU is a Jeskai-colored faction: red, white, blue.
- "America" is an internal design label for an ideological package: individual ambition, militarized civic identity, technological/social progress, and national mythmaking.
- Mechanical identity:
  - Permanents with gold counters get bonuses or unlock abilities.
  - Gold counters act as upgrades, medals, funding, patents, ranks, or civic investment.
  - The faction can use several mechanics rather than owning one narrow mechanic.

## Design Notes

- RWU should probably care about which objects have gold counters, not only total count.
- This differentiates it from WBU, which cares more about total wealth/institutions.
- RWU can make gold counters feel like enhancement, certification, promotion, or weaponization.
