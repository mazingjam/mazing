# Dictation 2026-06-16 002: WBR Faction

## Raw Notes

- WBR is the last civilization guild/faction.
- The theme is still uncertain.
- A recurring element may be Human Citizen tokens.
- Need suggestions for how to tie the faction together.

## Immediate Interpretation

- WBR is a Mardu-colored faction: white, black, red.
- It is one of the non-green civilization factions.
- Human Citizen tokens can be the faction's board material: population, mobs, workforce, voters, conscripts, worshippers, or debtors.

## Candidate Directions

### Mass Politics / Populism

- Citizens as crowds, rallies, movements, mobs, and public mandate.
- Can connect to Bribe through buying public support.
- Can connect to gold counters through campaign funding, wages, or patronage.

### Church / Public Order / Moral Authority

- Citizens as congregation, penitents, informants, and enforcers.
- Buildings as temples, courts, prisons, and civic halls.
- Can connect to Doom through martyrdom or death triggers.

### Labor / Debt / Exploitation

- Citizens as workers, debtors, contractors, and expendable bodies.
- Can connect to gold counters as wages/debt.
- Can connect to Bribe as coercion or paid loyalty.

## Mechanical Suggestions

- Create Human Citizen tokens.
- Sacrifice Citizens for value.
- Tap Citizens to Staff Buildings.
- Put gold counters on Citizens as wages, debts, favors, or marks of loyalty.
- Use Doom on cheap Citizens or cards that care when Citizens die.
- Bribe outcomes can create Citizen tokens or prevent Citizens from acting.
